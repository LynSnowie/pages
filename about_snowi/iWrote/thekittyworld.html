The Kitten Supurr Computer World Takeover




The third of February 2015

I was browsing the internet when I stumbled upon a website called “the Kitty takeover.world.org”. Intrigued, I decided to check it out, only to discover that cats weee plotting to take over the world! The header boldly says “We’re cats with a really powerful supurr computer and we’re going to take over the WORLD >;3”

Curious, I left a comment on the site asking, “I’m confused, do you have a super powerful supercomputer?” To my surprise, they replied just two minutes later with a simple “Yws.” As I pondered the absurdity of cats world-domination aspirations, I couldn’t help but find it oddly cute. Beneath the text, there was a picture depicting the cats alongside their formidable supercomputer.





The fourth of February 2015

The next day arrived, and the weight of the previous night’s revelation about the feline world-domination plot kept me from sleep. Suddenly, a sharp knock at my door disrupted the silence. Curious, I glanced at my video doorbell and beheld an unusual sight: four cats standing at my doorstep alongside a strange server-like contraption. Hesitantly, I opened the door and allowed the cats entry, unaware of the impending consequences.

As the cats made themselves comfortable in my living room, their intentions became clear. Assuming they were just ordinary cats, I greeted them cautiously. To my shock, they began to speak, revealing their truth, sinister nature. Stunned, I stuttered “Alright, who do you need ‘my’ help?” Before I could retract my words, as a sense of resignation washed over me as I surrendered to their demands with a defeated whisper, “Wait, never mind, I’ll help you.”. I move the supercomputer into my home server room

The cats, with their piercing stares, seemed to understand my surprise at their ability to communicate. One of them, a sleek black feline with piercing green eyes, stepped forward and explained in a surprisingly articulate voice, "We understand your confusion, Lyn. But we are not your ordinary cats. We possess intelligence far beyond what you humans comprehend."

Shocked and bewildered, I stumbled backward, struggling to process the reality of talking cats plotting world domination in my own home. Despite my initial disbelief, a sinking feeling of inevitability washed over me as I realised the gravity of the situation.

The lead cat, whom the others seemed to defer to, continued, "We require your assistance, Lyn, because your unique skills and knowledge are crucial to our plans. You possess access to technology and resources that can aid us in our quest for global supremacy."

Feeling a mixture of fear and resignation, I knew I had no choice but to comply. Suppressing my inner turmoil, I forced myself to adopt a facade of cooperation. "Very well," I muttered, my voice betraying my uncertainty. "What do you need me to do?"

The cats exchanged satisfied glances, their feline features contorted into unsettling grins. With a sense of foreboding, I braced myself for the role I had unwittingly been thrust into a reluctant accomplice in the cats' audacious scheme for world domination.



























I’ve lost track of time, it might be 2016?

My focus sharpened as I embarked on the monumental task of revamping the website. Amidst the whirlwind of coding, I dedicated myself to crafting a script of unprecedented power, a digital masterpiece hidden within the confines of the supurr computer's private storage.

With each line of code meticulously written and tested, I harnessed the combined might of Python, Java, C++, and other formidable languages, weaving them together into a tapestry of unparalleled complexity. The script, a digital colossus, lay dormant within the supurr computer, awaiting the moment of its awakening.

As days turned into weeks, I poured my heart and soul into perfecting the script, driven by an unrelenting desire to wield its power. Yet, even as I worked, a creeping sense of unease gnawed at the edges of my consciousness. The supurr computer, with its vast capabilities, held within it the potential for both greatness and destruction.

And then, on a fateful night, the script was complete. With trembling hands, I transferred it to the supurr computer's private storage, where it lay dormant, its true potential shrouded in secrecy. The weight of its existence hung heavy upon me, for I knew that with a single command, I could unleash unimaginable chaos upon the world.

But as I gazed upon the supurr computer, a sense of resolve welled up within me. Though the script held the power to reshape the world, it was ultimately my choice how to wield it. With determination in my heart, I vowed to use its power wisely, to steer humanity toward a brighter future.

And so, as I closed the lid of the supurr computer, I knew that the script would remain hidden, its secrets safeguarded from prying eyes. For now, its power lay dormant, waiting for the day when it would be called upon to fulfil its destiny.















The Takeover. December Thirty-first 2016

As the final hours of December Thirty-first 2016 slipped away, a quiet tension hung in the air, unnoticed by most but keenly felt by those who knew what was to come. In the dim glow of the supurr computer’s screen, Lyn meticulously prepared to unleash countless hours of coding and refinement, lay poised to execute its ultimate purpose.

But Lyn was not the only architect of this grand design. Lulu, Willow, Mittens, and Bingo, the four feline masterminds behind the plot, prowled the room with a sense of anticipation, their emerald eyes gleaming with a mixture of excitement and determination. With each passing moment, their plan drew closer to fruition, their dreams of world domination within reach.

At the stroke of midnight, the script sprang to life, its lines of code u furling like tendrils of shadow across the vast expanse of the internet. From servers to smartphones, from laptops to smart TVs, no corner of the digital world was safe from its influence. With each connection established, the script grew stronger, its algorithm adapting and evolving with a voracious hunger for power.

But the cats’ ambitions extended far beyond the confines of the digital realm. As news of the takeover spread like wildfire, panic swept through the streets, and humanity found itself ensnared In a web of fear and uncertainty. From the halls of government to bustling thoroughfares of every city, the cats’ influence was felt, their paw prints leaving an indelible mark upon the fabric of society.

Yet amidst the chaos, there were those dared to resist. Brave souls united in their defiance, rose up against the cats’ tyranny, their voices raised in a chorus of dissent. But their efforts were met with swift and merciless reprisal, as the cats, armed with cunning and intellect far surpassing that of their human adversaries, quashed any semblance of opposition with ruthless efficiency.

And so, as the first light of the new year dawned upon the world, humanity found itself under the rule of its feline overlords, their reign absolute and unchallenged. The takeover was complete, and as the echoes of celebration faded into the night, a new era began—one where cats reigned supreme, and the fate of humanity hung precariously in the balance.
